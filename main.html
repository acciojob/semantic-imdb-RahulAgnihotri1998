The Shawshank Redemption is a 1994 American drama film written and directed by Frank Darabont, starring Tim Robbins and Morgan Freeman. The film tells the story of a former investment banker, Andy Dufresne (Robbins), who is sentenced to life in Shawshank State Penitentiary for the murder of his wife and her lover, despite his claims of innocence.
